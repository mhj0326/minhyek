Machine Learning for Healthcare Applications

Hello, my name is Minhyek Jeon. I earned my bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Biotechnology from Korea University. Currently, I am pursuing a master’s degree in Computational Biology at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am working with Professor Fernando De La Torre in the Human Sensing Lab at the Robotics Institute. My primary focus has been on applying deep learning techniques to analyze medical data, aiming to uncover causal relationships and enhance clinical diagnostic workflows. Specifically, I have explored the use of text-to-image generative models for medical imaging data to strengthen model robustness.
